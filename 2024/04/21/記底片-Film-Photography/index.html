記底片 Film Photography

2023年年末我買了一臺柯達的M35傻瓜相機，輕巧的機身和懷舊的上片方式讓我想起了小時候擁有的第一臺底片相機。大概是已經太習慣使用數位單眼的關係，再次重新使用底片的過程只能以糟糕來形容，不知道為何就拍出了恐怖片的氛圍，卻也多了一點讓人懷念的色調和粗糙。

直到後來一遍遍的拍攝和沖洗以後，漸漸地理解了為什麼底片如此迷人，因為記憶也許會像相紙上沖洗出的影像褪色、發黃，但只要再次看到照片上凍結的時光，記憶就必然再次鮮明。

At the end of 2023, I bought a Kodak M35 point-and-shoot camera. Its lightweight body and nostalgic film format reminded me of the first film camera I owned as a child. Perhaps due to being too accustomed to using digital SLRs, the process of using film again could only be described as disastrous. For some reason, I managed to capture a horror movie atmosphere, yet it also added a touch of nostalgia with its tone and roughness.

It wasn’t until I repeatedly shot and developed film later on that I gradually understood why film is so enchanting. Memories may fade and yellow like images developed on photo paper, but as soon as I see the frozen moments captured in the photos again, the memories inevitably become vivid once more.
