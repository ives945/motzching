[ 關於我 About Me ]
本質上是一位文字創作者，攝影比較像是業餘的興趣，比起執著在字面上的身分，覺得自己更像是一個說書人或者獵人，無論是透過筆還是鏡頭都在試圖挖掘、狩獵更多的故事。過去作品多在Eyeem或粉專上分享，希望未來能讓更多人看到我所看見的事物。
I am a text creator. Photography is an amateur hobby. I feel more like a storyteller or a hunter than I am obsessed with literal identity. I am trying to dig out and hunt more stories whether through pen or lens. . In the past, my works were mostly shared on Eyeem or Facebook. I hope that more people can see what I see in the future.

[ 攝影理念 Photography concept]
透過攝影凝結剎那的時間，將我目光所見呈現在觀看者眼前，使觀看者同時成為被觀看者，進入那段逝去的時間當中，成為畫面中的一員。
Through the condensed moment of photography, I present what I see in front of the viewer, so that the viewer becomes the watched at the same time, enters the elapsed time and becomes a member of the picture.

[ 關鍵字 Keyword]
動物、微物、街拍
Animals, micro-objects, street photography
